NHL Trade Showcase Signal

Hypothesis: teams adjust a player's role — powerplay production, shot volume, ice time — in the weeks before trading them. The public NHL API doesn't expose per-game powerplay TOI for skaters, so this tracks a composite signal built from powerplay points, shots, and total TOI trend instead. Composite > 30 is flagged.
